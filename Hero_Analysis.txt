Heroes Of Pymoli Data Analysis

There were some trends in the data:

1. The item that most benefited the company was: Final Critic. Not only was the item purchased the most (13 times) 
   but it also had the greatest total purchase value ($59.99).

2. The vast majority of players were male, however, they spent the least per person, on average ($4.07). 
   Also largest age group 20-24 make up over 44%

3. Of the $2379.77 earned by the company, $1114.06 was earned on purchases made by players between 20 - 24 years old. 

The next highest spending age group was 15 - 19 years old ($412.89) and the 

third highest spenders were players between 25 and 29 years old ($293.00).
